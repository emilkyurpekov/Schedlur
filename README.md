Schedlur is a SaaS platform designed exclusively for appointment scheduling between businesses and their clients. We eliminate the hassle of back-and-forth communication, empowering organizations to manage bookings, reduce no-shows, and deliver a seamless customer experience.

### Key Features

- **Branded Booking Pages:** Share your custom link for instant appointments.
- **Smart Availability:** Set business hours, breaks, and holidays.
- **Automated Reminders:** Email & SMS confirmations and reminders.
- **Calendar Sync:** Integrate with Google, Outlook, and more.
- **Time Zone Detection:** Global-ready scheduling for your clients.
- **Client Management:** Keep track of appointments, histories, and notes.
- **Secure & Compliant:** Privacy-first, with enterprise-grade security.
